GRASS!

Grass is your dog's favorite vegetables.

They love to eat them uncooked and raw and they constantly look for them on their walks

Ingredients:

  • Seed
  • Soil
  • Sun

Steps:

  1. Take dog on a walk
  2. Let dog walk on any landscaping area with grass
  3. Dog will eat and chew on grass like a cow
  4. Dog might throw up grass due to upset stomach
  5. Dog has no regret and shame
